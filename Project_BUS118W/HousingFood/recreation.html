Sports Club

The Sport Club consists of a weight room equipped with both free weights and LifeFitness machines, as well as squat racks, treadmills, and recumbent bikes. In addition to the weight room, there is also a cardio hall equipped with elliptical machines, rowing machines, recumbent bikes, and stair climbers.

Bowling Center"

The Bowling Center is located in the lower level of the Student Union. This is a great place to relax between classes and play with friends. A popular billiards room is included in this facility, as well as a lounge area.

Spartan RecreationSRAC and Aquatic Center

When completed in Spring 2019, this building will provide additional gymnasiums, weight and fitness centers, exercise rooms, a rock climbing wall, and competition and recreation pools. The site is located at the southeast corner of 7th Street and San Carlos, across from the existing Event Center.
